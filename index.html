About the Artist

I'm Reagen Jessop, a Montanan artist that has been honing in my skills in painting and drawing with various mediums, including oil paints, pastels, and charcoal. I've loved to paint ever since I can remember. As a five year old I started doing potraits of people that I called "my girls". Portraits have always been my favorite thing to draw. As I got into highschool I progressed in my learning and started playing around with the meduims I wasn't familiar with, but now love to use. My inspiratiion comes from experiences I've had or concepts that I want to put into my language of art; to inspire and create those same emotions and experiences in the viewer. I want my viewers to feel deeply and live vicariously through my art.
